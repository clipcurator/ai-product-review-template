# AI Product Review Template

English | [简体中文](README.zh-CN.md)

Review templates for AI products covering use cases, strengths, limits, pricing checks, alternatives, and workflow fit.

This repository is a neutral, practical resource for AI product review writing, decision criteria, limitation notes, alternatives, and workflow-fit analysis. It is designed to help teams make clearer decisions, write better review notes, and avoid vague tool or workflow choices.

## Why This Repository Exists

AI product reviews are more helpful when they explain who the tool fits, where it struggles, what to verify, and which alternatives to compare. A template keeps reviews consistent and less promotional.

## Who This Is For

- AI review writers
- directory editors expanding tool pages
- SEO teams creating product review pages

## Core Workflow

1. Define the decision or review goal
2. Collect the required product, content, or workflow inputs
3. Use one template to make the evaluation comparable
4. Write fit, limits, and next-action notes
5. Refresh the document when products or workflows change

Start with the guide that matches your current decision point. Then copy one template into your own workspace, fill it in for one real project or product, and keep the notes for future comparison.
